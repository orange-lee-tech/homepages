# Registration

After entering [Baidu AI Cloud Academy](https://cloud.baidu.com/edu/edu_home/home), select Generative Artificial Intelligence Application Engineer – Junior to view the [Junior course details](https://cloud.baidu.com/edu/edu_home/certification/1),
select Generative Artificial Intelligence Application Engineer – Intermediate to view the [Intermediate course details](https://cloud.baidu.com/edu/edu_home/certification/2),
Generative Artificial Intelligence Application Engineer – Advanced, you can view the [Advanced course details](https://cloud.baidu.com/edu/edu_home/certification/3)

# Learning Format

Online self-paced learning: learning materials, lecture videos, and hands-on labs are provided. Learners can study via Baidu AI Cloud’s online platform, including watching video courses and using online materials. The program is delivered as online recorded courses with replay support. After enrollment starts, the courses automatically take effect, and learners can arrange their study time independently before the exam. Course validity period: 6 months from the start date of the class.

# Online Exam

Take the online exam within the specified time. The exam duration is 2 hours (generally scheduled on weekends). The paper includes two parts: objective questions (single-choice/multiple-choice) and practical tasks, each worth 100 points. A score of 80 is required to pass. The exam is an online closed-book exam; only a computer and an internet connection are needed. Screen switching is not supported, and switching browsers is not supported.
